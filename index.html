Common Questions

Is this considered cheating?

Think of it as a smart assistant. You're still answering — the AI helps you structure better, more confident answers. Many Fortune 500 candidates use similar tools.

Will my interviewer know I'm using it?

No. The extension runs locally in your browser. There's no indicator on the call, no screen sharing, nothing visible to the interviewer.

What platforms does it work on?

Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, Webex, and Slack Huddles. If it has a video call, the extension can detect the interviewer's questions.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your resume, notes, and interview data stay on your machine. Nothing is sent to third-party servers without your explicit consent.
